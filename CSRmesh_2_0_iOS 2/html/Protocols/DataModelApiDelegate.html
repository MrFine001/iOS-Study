– didReceiveStreamData:streamNumber:data:

didReceiveStreamData. Asynchronous callback upon receiving a packet of streamed data. More packets of data will follow until didReceiveStreamDataEnd. The receiver must assemble the parts in the correct order, as set by the stream number, to construct the whole.
