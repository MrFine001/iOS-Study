– ping:data:rspTTL:success:failure:

ping - ping the device with some data. The callback didPing is invoked upon success. The MeshServiceApi:didTimeoutMessage is invoked on failure.

- (NSNumber *)ping:(NSNumber *)deviceId data:(NSData *)data rspTTL:(NSNumber *)rspTTL success:(void ( ^ ) ( NSNumber *deviceId , NSData *arbitaryData , NSNumber *TTLAtRx , NSNumber *RSSIAtRx ))success failure:(void ( ^ ) ( NSError *error ))failure

failure
